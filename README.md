## 💼 Featured Case Studies

### ☕ F&amp;B Operations — Coffee Shop Sales Intelligence
`Category: Data Analytics & BI` · `Difficulty: Intermediate` · `Role: Data Analyst` · `Duration: 2 Weeks` · `Data Scale: 10,000 POS Transactions` · `Decision Supported: F&B Operations & Margin Strategy`

* **🔴 Business Challenge:** A retail café franchise operated blind. They suffered from high perishable inventory waste and unpredictable staffing bottlenecks due to a complete lack of visibility into peak hours and top-margin items from a dirty, raw 10,000-row transaction dataset.
* **🔵 Solution &amp; Methodology:** Structured and cleaned 95% of data anomalies using Excel UNIQUE/XLOOKUP. Engineered a Star Schema relational database in Power BI, writing complex DAX queries to dynamically segment peak sales periods and product margins.
* **📊 Business Impact:** 
  * Validated the **Pareto Principle** where the top 5 menu items generated 80% of total revenue ($84.7K baseline).
  * Provided combo bundle recommendations to pair high-margin slow-moving items with high-velocity items, cutting waste and optimizing sales.
  * Mapped hourly transaction volume, enabling management to adjust staff scheduling to meet actual peak demands.
* **🛠 Stack:** `Power BI` · `SQL` · `Excel`
* **🔗 Links:** [Live Power BI Dashboard](https://app.powerbi.com/view?r=eyJrIjoiMzYwMWU2ZGYtOTFmNy00NTEwLTk0MWEtYzY0OGM2NWM1OTU0IiwidCI6IjJiYjZlNWJjLWMxMDktNDdmYi05NDMzLWMxYzZmNGZhMzNmZiIsImMiOjl9) · [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/amr-mousa0/Coffee-Shop-Sales-Dashboard-Power-BI-) · [Read Full Case Study](https://github.com/amr-mousa0/Coffee-Shop-Sales-Dashboard-Power-BI-#readme)

### 📣 Digital Marketing — E-Commerce Campaign ROI Analytics
`Category: Marketing Analytics` · `Difficulty: Advanced` · `Role: Marketing & BI Analyst` · `Duration: 3 Weeks` · `Data Scale: Multi-Platform Ad Spends` · `Decision Supported: Marketing Budget Allocation`

* **🔴 Business Challenge:** The company spent marketing budgets across Facebook, Instagram, and Pinterest but had no unified way to track ROAS (Return on Ad Spend), CTR (Click-Through Rate), or CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) at a channel level, leading to budget waste during winter campaigns.
* **🔵 Solution &amp; Methodology:** Engineered a marketing analytics Star Schema in Power BI. Wrote custom DAX measures to calculate CPA, ROAS, and conversion trends, segmenting performance across seasonal variants and customer high-value clusters.
* **📊 Business Impact:**
  * Identified **Pinterest** as the highest ROI driver despite having lower click engagement, prompting a major budget reallocation.
  * Pinpointed Facebook as the brand awareness leader and optimized Meta campaigns to reduce overall cost-per-conversion.
  * Revealed critical winter campaign spend inefficiencies, enabling data-driven seasonal ad budget planning.
* **🛠 Stack:** `Power BI` · `Excel` · `Meta Ads Analytics`
* **🔗 Links:** [Live Power BI Dashboard](https://app.powerbi.com/view?r=eyJrIjoiZmQ1NmJmMWEtMmJhMy00YTg2LTg2NGItYTEzYWFhMjhiOWVmIiwidCI6IjJiYjZlNWJjLWMxMDktNDdmYi05NDMzLWMxYzZmNGZhMzNmZiIsImMiOjl9) · [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/amr-mousa0/Marketing-Campaigns-Analysis) · [Read Full Case Study](https://github.com/amr-mousa0/Marketing-Campaigns-Analysis#readme)

### 💰 Gym Franchise — Oxygen Gym Financial Transformation
`Category: Financial Analytics` · `Difficulty: Advanced` · `Role: Financial Data Analyst` · `Duration: 5 Weeks` · `Data Scale: 5 Years of Paper Ledger Records` · `Decision Supported: Strategic Financial Control`

* **🔴 Business Challenge:** A fitness franchise operated on 5 years of paper records. Management was suffering from severe billing leakages, unmonitored customer churn (80% monthly membership churn), and did not realize they had a declining -28% CAGR revenue trend.
* **🔵 Solution &amp; Methodology:** Digitized 5 years of paper records into a structured database. Built a robust Star Schema model in Power BI across three custom modules: Financial Analysis (CAGR, Break-Even Point), Product margins, and Membership overview.
* **📊 Business Impact:**
  * Identified and recovered **93,000 EGP** in leaked unbilled memberships.
  * Exposed a -28% CAGR decline, disproving the management's belief that cost-cutting alone was maintaining profitability.
  * Discovered a 43% loss margin on supplement sales and re-established profitable pricing tiers.
  * Delivered membership churn cohort models, leading to targeted retention strategy recommendations.
* **🛠 Stack:** `SQL` · `Excel` · `Power BI`
* **🔗 Links:** [Live Power BI Dashboard](https://app.powerbi.com/view?r=eyJrIjoiYzY5ZmVjZWUtYjg2NC00YWQ4LTllZGEtNDM4YjkzMjY2YTI1IiwidCI6IjJiYjZlNWJjLWMxMDktNDdmYi05NDMzLWMxYzZmNGZhMzNmZiIsImMiOjl9) · [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/amr-mousa0/Data-Analyst-Financial-Administrative-Data-) · [Read Full Case Study](https://github.com/amr-mousa0/Data-Analyst-Financial-Administrative-Data-#readme)
